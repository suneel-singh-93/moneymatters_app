Delete All App Data

Steps to Delete All User Data:

  1. Open the App.
  2. Go to the About Tab → Look for the Settings title.
  3. Scroll down and find the option: "Clear All Data and Cache".
  4. Click on it.
  5. Your app will restart with a fresh setup and all previous data will be removed.

Where to Find the Button

Click the red trash icon labeled “Clear All Data And Cache”.
